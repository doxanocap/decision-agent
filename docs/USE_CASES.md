# Use Cases & Examples

## 1. Career Decisions (High Stakes)
**Scenario**: Choosing between a stable corporate job and a risky startup founded by friends.
- **Input**: "The startup offers equity and freedom, but I fear failure. The corp job is boring but safe."
- **Analysis**:
  - *Bias Detected*: Status Quo Bias, Fear of Missing Out (FOMO).
  - *RAG Context*: "In 2022, you regretted not taking a risk on Project X."
  - *Recommendation*: Evaluates the "Safety vs Growth" tradeoff based on your historical preference for growth.

## 2. Relocation Dilemmas
**Scenario**: Moving to a new country for better quality of life vs staying near family.
- **Input**: "I want to move to Berlin for the tech scene, but my parents are aging."
- **Analysis**:
  - *Logical Fallacy*: False Dichotomy (assuming you can't visit or support them remotely).
  - *Score*: 75/100 (Strong emotional grounding, but improved logistical reasoning needed).

## 3. Financial Investments
**Scenario**: Buying a house vs investing in stocks.
- **Input**: "Rent is throwing money away. Buying builds equity."
- **Analysis**:
  - *Bias Detected*: Sunk Cost Fallacy (rent is paying for shelter, not 'loss').
  - *Data Check*: "Rent vs Buy calculator suggestions needed."

## 4. Personal Relationships
**Scenario**: Confronting a toxic friend vs ghosting.
- **Input**: "I don't want conflict, so I'll just stop replying."
- **Analysis**:
  - *Weakness*: Avoidance coping mechanism identified.
  - *Past Context*: "You valued 'Direct Communication' in your 2023 resolutions."
